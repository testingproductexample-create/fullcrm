# Research Plan: Inventory Management for Tailoring Businesses

## Research Objectives
Research comprehensive inventory management strategies specifically for tailoring businesses, covering:
- Fabric stock management systems
- Supplier tracking and relationship management
- Material ordering processes
- Cost calculation methods
- Fabric categorization (wool, cotton, silk, etc.)
- Color matching systems
- Pattern management
- Inventory alerts for low stock
- Supplier relationship management
